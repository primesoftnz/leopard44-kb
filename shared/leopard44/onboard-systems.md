# Leopard 44 / Sunsail 444 — Onboard Systems

## Electrical System

### Batteries
- **Standard:** 2x 180Ah AGM house + 2x 96Ah engine starter
- **Owner upgrades:** 3x 210–215Ah Lifeline AGM house batteries
- **Modern upgrades:** 600Ah lithium-ion banks (e.g., Relion)

### Charging and Inverters
- High-output alternators (12V 80A Hitachi) on main engines
- Shore power: dual 30A inlets
- Battery chargers: 40A (e.g., Victron Centaur)
- Inverters: Mastervolt or GoPower 2,000–3,000W

### Solar
- Common upgrade: panels on hardtop bimini
- Configurations range from 2x 75W to 400W+ arrays
- MPPT controllers standard for upgraded systems

## Plumbing and Water

### Freshwater
- Standard: 206 US gal (780 L)
- Charter versions: up to 264 gal (1,000 L) in three tanks
- Hot water: ~12 gal (45 L) cylinders

### Black Water
- Holding tanks: 26 US gal (100 L) total, split into two 13-gal tanks
- 4x Jabsco large-bowl electric macerator flush heads

### Watermaker
- Not standard; optional fit
- Capacity up to 40 gal/hour on some installations

## Engines and Saildrives

- **Standard:** 2x 29hp Yanmar diesel
- **Optional:** 2x 39hp Yanmar (model 3JH5E) — recommended for head seas
- **Saildrives:** Yanmar SD50 or SD60 with 2-blade fixed props
- **Access:** Large watertight Lewmar deck hatches (not fiberglass covers), sound insulated

## Generator

- Optional Northern Lights 6kW or 9kVA
- Housed in forward deck locker
- Standard on charter versions

## Climate Control

### Air Conditioning
- Cruisair systems: 2x 16,000 BTU or combined 40,000 BTU
- Soft-start technology, sound-insulating enclosures

### Refrigeration
- Vitrifrigo drawer-style front-opening fridge/freezer
- Capacity: ~56 gal (212 L)
- Some owners add standalone fridge/freezer chests

## Galley Appliances

- 3-burner Force 10 gas stove and oven
- Double stainless steel sink
- Corian countertops
- Dedicated washer/dryer locker in owner's version

## Navigation and Electronics

- **Chartplotters:** Raymarine C90w, e7, or Axiom displays
- **Instruments:** ST60+ or i50/i60 depth, wind, speed
- **VHF:** Ray 55
- **Autopilot:** Raymarine ST6002+ or similar
- All sail controls led to 3x Lewmar winches at raised starboard helm

## Charter vs Owner Configuration

| Feature | Charter (Sunsail 444) | Owner's Version |
|---|---|---|
| Cabins | 4 cabin, 4 head | 3 cabin, 3 head (starboard = master suite) |
| Generator + A/C | Standard | Optional |
| Lithium batteries | No | Common upgrade |
| Watermaker | Rarely | Common upgrade |
| Washer/dryer | No | Dedicated locker provided |
