# Leopard 44 / Sunsail 444 — Technical Specifications

The Leopard 44 is a high-performance cruising catamaran built by **Robertson and Caine** in South Africa and designed by the American team of **Gino Morrelli and Pete Melvin**. It pioneered the **forward cockpit** design with direct access from the saloon.

## Dimensions and Measurements

| Measurement | Metric | Imperial |
|---|---|---|
| Length Overall (LOA) | 12.98 m | 42 ft 7 in |
| Length at Waterline (LWL) | 12.7 m | 41 ft 8 in |
| Beam | 7.25 m | 23 ft 9 in |
| Draft | 1.27 m | 4 ft 2 in |
| Mast Height | 21.00 m | 68 ft 11 in |
| Headroom (saloon) | 2.12 m | ~6 ft 11 in |

## Displacement and Tonnage

- **Light Displacement:** 12,620 kg / 27,811 lbs
- **Tested/Operational Displacement:** 27,880–32,930 lbs (varies by source)
- **EC-Type Loaded Displacement:** 17,180 kg

| Mainsail | 74 | 797 |

| Gennaker (optional) | 130 | 1,399 |
| **Total Standard** | — | 1,270–1,323 |

The mast is positioned forward to favor the mainsail.

## Engine and Mechanical

- **Standard Engines:** 2x 29 hp Yanmar diesel
- **Optional Engines:** 2x 39 hp Yanmar diesel
- **Drive Type:** Saildrives (Yanmar SD50/SD60)
- **Propellers:** 2-blade fixed (standard)
- **Generator (optional):** Northern Lights 6kW or 9kVA, housed in forward deck locker

## Tank Capacities

| Tank | Volume |
|---|---|
| Fuel | 185 US gal / 700 L (two tanks) |
| Water | 206 US gal / 780 L (charter versions up to 1,000 L in three tanks) |
| Holding (waste) | 26 US gal / 100 L total (two 13-gal tanks) |

## Construction Details

- **Hull and Deck:** GRP sandwich, vacuum-bonded balsa core
- **Resin:** Vinylester resin with isophthalic gelcoat (osmosis protection)
- **Bulkheads:** Watertight crash bulkheads in bows and engine rooms
- **Windows:** 12mm Perspex cast acrylic (coachroof and forward cockpit door); tempered glass companionway doors
- **Interior Finish:** Cherry laminate/veneer, maple or imitation oak soles, Corian galley countertops

## Layout and Accommodations

- **3-Cabin Owner's Version:** Entire starboard hull is master suite (queen berth aft, study/sofa amidships, large head/shower forward)
- **4-Cabin Charter Version (Sunsail 444):** Four double cabins, each with en-suite head
- **Berths:** Up to 10 people (including converted saloon and forepeak berths)
- **Heads:** 3 or 4 en-suite depending on configuration

## CE Certification

- **Category A (Ocean)** rated
