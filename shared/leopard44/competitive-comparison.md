## Value and Reputation

- **Leopard 44:** Exceptional brokerage value, quick sales near asking price. Won Boat of the Year from SAIL and Cruising World (2012). Reputation for sturdiness and low maintenance (charter fleet proven).
- **Lagoon:** Market leader in volume, highly available secondhand, less performance-oriented
- **Fountaine Pajot (Lucia 40):** Well-canvassed, light, sails better than Lagoons; lacks forward cockpit innovation
- **Bali 4.3:** Maximizes occupancy, common in charter fleets
- **Catana:** Premium price point, performance focus, smaller production numbers

## Key Leopard 44 Differentiators

1. **Forward cockpit with direct saloon access** — pioneered for production cats, now industry trend
2. **Charter fleet proven** — designed for minimal downtime and long-term durability
3. **Single-point sail handling** — all controls led to protected starboard helm
4. **Value retention** — owner's versions sell quickly near asking price
