# Leopard 44 / Sunsail 444 — Recommended Upgrades and Modifications

## Electrical System Upgrades

- **Lithium battery conversion:** 600Ah lithium-ion banks (e.g., Relion) replacing AGM
- **Inverter/charger upgrade:** 3,000W units (e.g., Victron Multiplus) with dedicated battery monitoring
- **Solar expansion:** 400W+ arrays with MPPT controllers on hardtop bimini

## Rigging and Sail Handling

- **Mainsheet traveler conversion:** Add traditional traveler or upgrade the twin-sheet (Delta) system
- **Headsail upgrade:** Replace non-overlapping jib with overlapping genoa for light-air performance
- **Light air / downwind sails:** Install bowsprit for Code 0 or asymmetrical spinnaker
- **Power assistance:** WinchRite electric handle for hoisting heavy mainsail

## Structural Reinforcements and Safety

- **Sail drive fairing fix:** Replace rubber boots with custom fiberglass "clamshell" panels for permanent seal
- **Trampoline replacement:** Upgrade OEM nets to 3/8" Offshore netting (higher openness prevents water capture and blowouts)
- **Forward cockpit protection:** Conical splash cover or specialized canvas for offshore passages
- **Rigging inspection:** Reinforce/check forestay and gooseneck fixing points, steering cables

## Mechanical and Plumbing

- **Sail drive upgrade:** SD50 to SD60 units (avoids known clutch cone issues)
- **Header tank:** For sail drive lubricant — relieves pressure in hot climates, protects seals
- **Watermaker:** 30–40 gal/hour unit — essential for extended cruising

## Comfort and Quality of Life

- **Isinglass enclosures:** For helm station and rear cockpit — weather protection in rain/spray
- **Washing machine:** Utilizes dedicated locker in owner's version
- **Caframo fans:** Replace standard fans in all cabins
- **Storage optimization:** Replace starboard hull sofa with custom cabinetry (owner's version)
