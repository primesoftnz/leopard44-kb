# Leopard 44 / Sunsail 444 — Bluewater Capability

CE Category A (Ocean) rated — built for significant wind and sea conditions on extended offshore passages.

## Safety Features

- **Crash boxes:** Watertight bulkheads in lower bows isolate collision damage
- **Engine room isolation:** Watertight bulkheads
- **Forward cockpit drainage:** Two oversized drain holes empty cockpit in <45 seconds
- **Wing bridge design:** Wedge-shaped forward edge sheds water onto weather decks
- **Forward door:** Dog clamps for submarine-like watertight seal in heavy seas
- **Hull construction:** Vacuum-bonded GRP sandwich with end-grain balsa core (prevents moisture wicking)

## Provisioning and Capacity

- Large forepeak storage areas accessible through deck hatches or forward cabin doors
- In-floor storage bins in galley for large items
- Extensive cabinetry and hanging lockers throughout
- **Load Carrying Capacity:** ~4,560 kg (~10,000 lbs) for fuel, water, gear, crew
- Overloading degrades performance and reduces bridgedeck clearance (slamming risk)

## Range Under Power

- **Fuel capacity:** 185 US gal (700 L)
- **Cruising speed:** ~6 knots at 2,600 rpm; ~8 knots at 2,800 rpm
- **Consumption:** ~1 gal/hour at cruise on single engine
- **Estimated range:** ~1,100 nautical miles (single engine motoring)

## Real-World Passage Experience

### Cape Town to Caribbean Deliveries
- Standard delivery route: 5,000+ miles across South Atlantic
- 45-day journey; reported as handled with ease
- Spray deflects away from forward cockpit

### Offshore Performance
- Extremely stable platform in varied weather including squalls
- Off-the-wind: easily 9+ knots
- Upwind pointing reduced when reefed or jib furled

## Passage Preparation Checklist

1. **Rigging:** Inspect forestay, gooseneck fixing points, steering cables
2. **Trampolines:** Upgrade to 3/8" Offshore netting (prevents green water capture)
3. **Engines:** Consider 39hp upgrade for head seas
4. **Watermaker:** Install for off-grid self-sufficiency
5. **Solar:** Expand array on hardtop (reduce generator dependence)
6. **Weather protection:** Conical splash cover for forward cockpit, isinglass enclosures for helm
7. **Communications:** SSB/satellite, AIS, radar
